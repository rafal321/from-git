------
dd		cut line
p		paste line, under cursor
de		delete till next word
di"		delete within " "
ci"		delete within " " , with insert mode
------

d,$		delete till end of line
D		del till end of line
------
gg		top    of file
G		bottom of file
ctrl + b 	page upb0
ctrl + f 	page down
w		move to next word	
b		move to prev word
0		move to beg of line	
$		move to end of line
------
u		undo
v		highlight + d  to delete
------
0D		delete characters in a line

ma		mark
d'm		delete to this mark
